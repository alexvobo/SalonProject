Conveniently located in the heart of NYC’s Financial District, American Beauty provides affordable luxury to men and women in an elegant, professional environment. We get into and out of your hair leaving you looking and feeling great!

Our goal here at American Beauty has always been to introduce the best hairstylists in both downtown and uptown NYC. Those who remember Salon K2 and Karena’s Kuts already know the service and dedication we provide. With over 15 years experience of serving the Financial District, we at American Beauty will continue to do nothing but the best.

So men... relax in comfort! Get the best haircut ever! Women… you will be treated with the inherent respect for beauty that all great salons should always possess. We are experts in hair techniques including balayage painting, ombre, keratin smoothing, hair relaxing, permanent waves, blow drying, deep conditioning, and corrective services.
